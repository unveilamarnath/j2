Common Questions

Fee FAQs

Can I enrol my child for just one subject?
−
Yes. Jaagrut™ has subject-based pricing. You can enrol for Maths only, Science only, or any combination. There is no pressure to take all four subjects.
Is the ₹250 assessment fee refunded if I don't enrol?
+
The ₹250 is not refunded if you choose not to enrol — but you do receive a live faculty session, which is of real value in itself. If you do enrol, the ₹250 is fully adjusted against your course fee.
Do you have monthly instalment plans?
+
Annual fee is the standard structure. Monthly instalment plans are available on request. Please mention your preference when you fill the fee enquiry form.
Is there a sibling discount?
+
Yes. If you are enrolling two children, please mention it in the enquiry form and we will factor in the sibling discount before you finalise.
