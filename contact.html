Quick Answers

Questions parents ask us most

Are classes live or pre-recorded?
−
Our sessions are conducted live online. Limited offline options are also available — reach out to us to learn more.
What if my child misses a class?
+
For group batches, recorded sessions are not provided by default; however, sessions can be recorded upon request. We recommend reaching out to our counsellor to explore the best option for your child.
How much time does a child need to give each day?
+
Typically 1–3 hours a day, depending on your child's grade and learning goals. A detailed schedule is shared with parents at the start of the programme. For a personalised estimate, speak with a Jaagrut counsellor.
Is there a free trial?
+
Yes! For 1-on-1 sessions, we offer a free 30-minute trial. For group batches, if your child joins from Day 1, you get 15 days free before any payment is due. If your child joins mid-way through a topic, learning begins from the next fresh topic with a 7-day free trial — note that a prior assessment is required, which carries a nominal fee of ₹250.
Do group classes offer individual attention?
+
We do our best to track every student's progress in each class. That said, a group session is inherently different from a 1-on-1 class for personalized attention.
