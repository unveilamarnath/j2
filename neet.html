Our NEET Approach

NEET Biology needs
real understanding, not just memory

NEET has 90 Biology questions. Most students approach Biology as a memorisation subject. That works for Class 12 boards — it is not enough for NEET, where application and reasoning are tested.

At Jaagrut™, we teach NCERT Biology in full depth — so students understand processes, not just definitions. Physics and Chemistry are taught with the same concept-first discipline applied across all our classes.
