Our Story

Small by choice.
Personal by design.

Seventeen years ago, Jaagrut™ started in Bengaluru with a simple idea: that a student who truly understands a subject will never have to fear an exam.

Not a groundbreaking idea. But a hard one to actually stick to when the pressure is to cover portions, finish the syllabus, and move on. We chose not to move on. Not until the concept was clear. Not until the student could explain it back, not just repeat it.

That meant keeping batches small. It meant talking to parents honestly, even when the news was not good. It meant building lesson plans and worksheets that addressed gaps instead of padding over them. None of it was the fastest way to run tutoring practice. But it was the only way we knew how to do it right.

Over the years, we saw what that approach produced. Students who stopped being afraid of Maths. Students who walked into board exams feeling prepared, not just hopeful. Parents who finally felt like someone was being straight with them about where their child stood.

We are fully online now. But the batch size is still 15. Parents still hear from us directly. The worksheets, the Prerequisite tests, the honest conversations, all of it is still here. Because the idea has not changed. And we have no plans to change it.
